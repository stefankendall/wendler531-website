Wendler 5/3/1

Forget the calculator. Make it easy.

Follow Jim Wendler's 5/3/1 lifting program to increase strength and lifting ability. Open the app, set your maxes, and get lifting.

Features:

  • See Wendler lift numbers by week and lift
  • Estimate one-rep max from known lifts
  • Optionally show warm-up sets
