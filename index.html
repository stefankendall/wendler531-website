Wendler 5/3/1

Follow Jim Wendler's 5/3/1 lifting program to increase strength and lifting ability. Get the app, and get lifting.

Features:

  • See Wendler lift numbers by week and lift!
  • Estimate one-rep max from known lifts!
  • Add new lifts, like Clean or Snatch!
